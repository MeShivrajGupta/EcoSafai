6-Bin Waste Segregation

Proper waste segregation helps recycling, reduces landfill, and promotes a cleaner, greener environment. Har ghar se shuru ho skta hai badlav.

Wet Waste

Biodegradable kitchen and garden waste.

  • 🍌 Fruit Peels
  • 🥗 Vegetable Scraps
  • 🍞 Leftover Food
  • 🍂 Garden Leaves

Dry Waste

Recyclable household items.

  • 📦 Cardboard
  • 📰 Newspapers
  • 🥤 Plastic Bottles
  • 🍾 Glass Jars

Hazardous Waste

Materials dangerous for health & environment.

  • 🔋 Batteries
  • 💡 CFL Bulbs
  • 🧴 Chemicals
  • 💊 Expired Medicines

E-Waste

Discarded electronic devices and parts.

  • 💻 Old Computers
  • 📱 Broken Phones
  • 🔌 Cables
  • 🖨️ Printers

Sanitary Waste

Personal hygiene waste.

  • 🩸 Sanitary Pads
  • 👶 Diapers
  • 🧻 Tissue Papers
  • 💉 Medical Cotton

Biodegradable Waste

Natural waste that decomposes quickly.

  • 🌿 Garden Grass
  • 🌽 Corn Husks
  • ☕ Coffee Grounds
  • 🍂 Dry Leaves
